Rapid Rafting

Here at Rapid Rafting, we give customers an experience they will never forget offering fun water rafting rides accompanied by professional help to insure their safety. We assure you that this will be the ride of a lifetime.

History

Rapid Rafting was founded in a small island in Tonga back in 1950 by a family who wanted to share the gifts of the island with others. Starting off with nothing else but a dream and a passion for adventure, the company has grown to become the biggest white water rafting company in the entire pacific.
